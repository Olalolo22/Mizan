**Project Title:**
Mizan 

**Description:**
Mizan is an institutional-grade infrastructure layer for tokenizing Real World Assets (Ijarah Sukuk) on Arc. It utilizes SP1 zkVM to verify CPA attestations completely off-chain, ensuring high-net-worth investors can hold fractional, USDC-yielding assets without exposing identity or documents on-chain. Continuous embedded compliance guarantees secondary market tokens can only be transferred between ZK-verified wallets.
